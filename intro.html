Web of Trust Introduction + PGP Keysigning Party

19:00, 10 June 2019

Trust, Kluckstr. 25, 10785 Berlin

Sarah Friend, Cory Levinson, Sam Hart

Bring your laptop!

This intro will include presentations about what constitutes a Web of Trust and a breif history of PGP (Pretty Good Privacy), tracing its origins to the early cypherpunk movement.

Afterward, we will hold a PGP Keysigning Party to get hands-on experience with the Web of Trust system.
